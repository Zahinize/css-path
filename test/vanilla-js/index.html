    On The House
    C Scheme, Jaipur
    Anurag Mohanty
    11 Reviews , 40 Followers
    Follow
    19 hours ago via Zomato for Android
    Rated
      
    The last eating place of my entire Rajasthan trip of one week was indeed worth the wait I guess. After being disappointed in the morning at the much hyped Niros, I got back my faith with the offerings of Jaipur at this place. Truely continental as they say. It is located just by the main road of C scheme area in the Ramesh Marg. It may not be available on Google maps, but can be navigated via Zomato map.

    Ambience: OTH indeed gives the feeling of bistro as you read in the description. Wooden interiors and furniture give it a classy feel. Coloured water bottles with a cork like opening style is a good add-on.

    Service: The waiters and stewards are courteous and well informed about the cuisine. They will make sure you are occupied and well settled throughout the experience.

    Food: I had ordered a Mexican chicken burrito and Chicken stuffed with bacon in zaferano sauce. They serve two burritos on a plate for a single order. My brother kept on munching till he felt filled up midway of the second piece. They are heavy filled with ample amount of Mayo, chicken, rajma, rice grains and some more pulses. The wraps were especially authentic. Then came my magical Chicken stuffed with bacon. They only reason I did not give them a full five was because of the time taken for the 2nd dish. It was more than 15mins to prepare it which I thought was not usual. I would also like to add that it was sort of worthy because of the treat to my taste buds. The chicken was succulent and tender whereas the stuffed bacon was still partly crispy which took it to the next level. The zafrani sauce had a distinct flavour of zafran or kesar. It was a light yellow thick creamy cheesy gravy. It had about 6 round boneless pieces of chicken with bacon at the centre. The mashed potatoes complimented them well. The entire dish was garnished with finely chopped parsley which gave a nice aroma as well as flavour to the dish. Even, I felt filled up by the time I reached upto the last chunk of chicken.

    Overall, its a must go for every foodie who loves continental and happens to be in Jaipur even for a day.
